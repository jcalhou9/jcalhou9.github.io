The Student Dreamer

The Student Dreamer is a brand dedicated to inspiring individuals to pursue their dreams, no matter the obstacles or the time it takes. Our mission is to remind people that "Time does not wait for dreams," so neither should they. We believe it's never too late to learn, grow, and chase what truly matters. The journey toward your dreams should be embraced with courage and action. Whether you're just starting out or considering a new direction, don't overthink it, take that step now. The Student Dreamer is here to encourage you as you turn your dreams into reality.

—Colors—

  • Background - The background is going to be #4a494f which is a grey.
  • Header and Footer - The header and footer are going to have the same design with a #2f2f5d99 background which is a blueish purple and 60% opacity along with a #e4e0c1 box shadow.
  • Link Buttons - The link buttons are going to follow the same design of the header and footer except for a #5b65db99 hover which is a lighter purple and a page identifer box shadow #00e2ff which is a teal.

—Font—

  • Paragraph Font - The Paragraph and list fonts are going to be set to Comic Sans MS, Comic Sans, cursive.
  • Headings Font - All of the headings will have a georgia font and h1 headings will be italicized.
